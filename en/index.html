Notes

  • Shipping: We cover shipping (Letter Pack Light) if the final appraisal exceeds ¥10,000.
  • If rare cards and normal cards are mixed, please pack them separately in advance (otherwise they may be treated as normal cards).
  • Separating by rarity helps speed up appraisal.
  • Duel Masters: VR and above are ¥10 per card.
  • Large-volume appraisals for the same title may be reduced.
  • Appraisal may be reduced for scratches/chips on either side.
  • Severe damage (tears, writing, water damage, sun fading, major bends/warping, etc.) may be valued at ¥0.
  • Counterfeit/unauthorized items are “return only (no appraisal).”
  • Buyback prices may change without notice due to market and stock conditions.
  • We do not deduct shipping or fees from the appraisal amount.
  • We may remove cards from sleeves/files for condition checks (we cannot restore the original arrangement).
  • Items with strong odors (tobacco, perfume, etc.) may be refused.
